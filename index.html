An aptitude test is a systematic means of testing a job candidate's abilities to perform specific tasks and react to a range of different situations. The tests each have a standardised method of administration and scoring. The results are quantified and compared with all other test takers.

At SmartFuture we strive to provide each participant with the best preparation for their needs.

Our goal is to try to provide tailored preparation for every eligible online or in person resource that uses psychometric selection tests and to ensure that this assessment is up-to-date with the most recent preparation material.
