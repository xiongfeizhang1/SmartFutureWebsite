Research Focused

In addition to offering assessments and self-discovery resources to our visitors, we offer the opportunity to participate in research studies examining how personality factors relate to everything from income to parenting style. Our research has been covered in major media outlets including Business Insider, the Huffington Post, and the Today Show.

Accessibility is a top priority for us, as we seek to ensure that our services are available to those who need them most. Through our program for nonprofit organizations, we are proud to provide free assessment services to a wide range of at-risk populations, from foster children to military veterans.
