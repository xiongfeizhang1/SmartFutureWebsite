What is SmartFuture?

SmartFuture is created by university students who understand how difficult finding the right career is for students. At SmartFuture, we hope by using our own personal experiences and reviewed sources, such as United States' General Aptitude Test Battery and Canada's Applicant Testing Services, we're able to create an assessment for users that can reliably show them their strengths. We hope by illustrating their all of strengths, and displaying information about careers complementing their traits, the students can use our information to find the career that suits them the best.
